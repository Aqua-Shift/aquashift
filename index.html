AquaSHIFT

The excess of water during flood is harmful and so is the deficit of it, in a drought-struck zone. Minimizing the damage calls for a sophisticated approach, where we can shift the surplus water from a flood prone zone for storage and later usage in harsh weather conditions.

OUR SOLUTION

Using AquaSHIFT we suggest the possible links between places which lie below the maximum feasible distance for transfer. These links can be studied and analyzed in depth, to assess the viability of the solution, constrained to the terrain and climate.
